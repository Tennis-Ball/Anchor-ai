What is Anchor?

Anchor is an online platform that aims to provide its users with accurate machine learning predictions on stock market prices. Anchor provides a predicted closing price for today as well as the accuracy rating and expected price changes.

How does it work?

Behind the scenes Anchor runs a training program that takes in the symbol's entire history of open prices, close prices, volumes, daily highs, and daily lows to output a predicted closing price for today. It trains a multiple regression model with a large neural network for 10 iterations for each symbol to provide the most accurate predictions.
